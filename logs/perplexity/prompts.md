# Perplexity prompts

These are the standalone domain prompts sent to Perplexity. Daily response logs remain paired with the exact prompt used for that run.

## Crypto

Search current news, official sources, exchange filings, regulatory announcements, and credible financial reporting from the last 48 hours on cryptocurrency markets, DeFi, and blockchain infrastructure. Prioritize SEC filings, central bank statements, exchange announcements, and on-chain data reports over opinion pieces. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where regulatory language has shifted, where institutional filings contradict public narrative, where multiple unrelated sources reference the same wallet activity or liquidity move without connecting it explicitly, or where government silence on a major move is unusual.

## Stocks / Markets

Search current news, official sources, earnings filings, Federal Reserve communications, and credible financial reporting from the last 48 hours on equity markets and macro economy. Prioritize SEC filings, earnings releases, central bank minutes, treasury announcements, and IMF/World Bank reports over media commentary. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where official economic data contradicts market movement, where insider filing activity is unusual, where multiple sectors are moving in the same direction without an obvious shared cause, or where a major institution has gone quiet on something they were recently vocal about.

## AI / Tech Breakthroughs

Search current news, official sources, research publications, patent filings, and credible reporting from the last 48 hours on artificial intelligence, emerging technology, and major tech company moves. Prioritize arxiv preprints, official company announcements, government AI policy documents, and university research releases over tech journalism. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where a capability announcement contradicts recent public statements, where government bodies are quietly moving on AI regulation, where military or defense adjacent organizations are funding or acquiring AI tools, or where the same breakthrough is being referenced across unrelated research groups simultaneously.

## Energy / Advanced Science

Search current news, official sources, research publications, energy department filings, and credible reporting from the last 48 hours on energy innovation including fusion, advanced fission, grid technology, and materials science. Prioritize DOE announcements, IAEA reports, patent filings, university research releases, and company funding disclosures over science journalism. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where funding quietly shifted toward or away from a specific technology, where a government is making energy infrastructure moves that haven’t been widely reported, where materials breakthroughs are appearing in multiple unrelated labs simultaneously, or where energy policy language has changed without a public announcement.

## Geopolitics / Conflict

Search current news, official government sources, military announcements, diplomatic communications, and credible reporting from the last 48 hours on geopolitics, active conflict zones, and international power shifts. Prioritize UN statements, government press releases, verified journalist reports from regional outlets, and think tank publications over pundit commentary. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where official government statements contradict verified ground reports, where troop or naval movements are documented but not publicly acknowledged, where diplomatic language has quietly shifted, where commodity prices are moving in ways that suggest insider knowledge of a coming event, or where a major power has gone unusually silent.

## Major Companies / Business Intel

Search current news, official sources, SEC filings, earnings calls, merger filings, and credible business reporting from the last 48 hours on major company strategy, acquisitions, leadership changes, and market moves. Prioritize SEC 8-K and 13-F filings, FTC/DOJ antitrust communications, official press releases, and verified executive statements over business media. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where a filing contradicts recent public statements, where a major executive quietly changed position or left, where antitrust activity is accelerating in a specific sector, where multiple large companies are making similar moves simultaneously without an obvious shared reason, or where a company has gone quiet right before a historically active period.

## Demographics / Consumer Behavior / Cultural Signal

Search current news, official sources, census data releases, retail filings, and credible demographic reporting from the last 48 hours on population movement, consumer spending patterns, and cultural behavior shifts. Prioritize Census Bureau releases, BLS consumer expenditure data, retail earnings filings, university demographic research, and migration pattern reports over lifestyle journalism. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where official population data contradicts real estate or retail movement patterns, where consumer spending in one category is surging while another collapses without an obvious shared cause, where migration patterns are accelerating toward or away from specific regions in ways not reflected in housing price data yet, or where cultural behavior shifts are appearing across multiple unrelated demographic groups simultaneously.

## Supply Chain / Logistics / Infrastructure

Search current news, official sources, port authority filings, shipping data, and credible infrastructure reporting from the last 48 hours on global supply chains, logistics networks, and physical infrastructure. Prioritize US DOT filings, port authority reports, freight rate indices, Army Corps of Engineers infrastructure data, FAA and FRA operational reports, and manufacturing output data over business media. Summarize the top 10 developments ranked by significance. Flag anything where freight rates are moving without an obvious demand explanation, where port congestion or rerouting is appearing at multiple unconnected locations simultaneously, where infrastructure failure or maintenance reports are clustering in a specific region or sector, where manufacturing output data contradicts inventory data in ways that suggest hidden supply pressure, or where government infrastructure spending documents reveal priorities not reflected in public infrastructure announcements.
