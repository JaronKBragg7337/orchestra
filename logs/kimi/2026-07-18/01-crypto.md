Insider trading limits, stronger disclosure, and prison terms for unregistered operators raised from 3 to 10 years. Flat 20% crypto gains tax locked in from 2028, replacing rates up to 55%. ~13.47M exchange accounts as of end-2025 . This is the biggest "Eastern hemisphere goes its own way" signal of the week — Tokyo is tightening into legitimacy while Washington deregulates.
